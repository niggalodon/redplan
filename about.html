Who are we?

What is RedPLAN?

RedPLAN Consultants is a female-owned Social Enterprise founded in 2013. We support Small and Micro-Enterprises to develop practical business solutions in order to grow their businesses sustainably.

What we do

We are skilled in Business Development, Training Facilitation, Coaching and Mentorship as well as Digital Marketing.

We have trained over 500 entrepreneurs and solopreneurs across various fields supporting initiatives in both urban and rural communities.

We focus on

More specifically, our entrepreneurship support focuses on training Startups from idea to action, supporting them through their compliance requirements, development of Business Plans, Writing Marketing plans, Mentorship, Coaching and Financial Management.

We are driven by

RedPLAN is driven by the desire to empower entrepreneurs in order to grow their businesses and create sustainable employment opportunities.

Our Secret Sauce

Our secret sauce lies in our training methodology and delivery focusing on practical, experiential and introduction of simple business practices which address specific pain points for individual businesses.

We are also digital

In addition to SMME training, we also offer digital and content development services for companies and individuals looking to build their social media presence.
